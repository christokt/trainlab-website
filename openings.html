We welcome motivated students interested in AI, wireless communication, optimization, and Bayesian inference.

PhD Openings

We will have openings for 1–2 PhD students during 2025–2026. We are looking for highly motivated and talented students with a strong background in at least two areas among AI, wireless communication, mathematical optimization, or Bayesian inference.

WPI has rolling admission, so students are welcome to apply anytime during the year.

How to apply

Please reach out with your CV, publications if any, and transcripts.

We are especially interested in students passionate about reshaping the future of wireless communications through rigorous research and innovation.
