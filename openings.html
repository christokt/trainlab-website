PhD Openings

We will have openings for 1–2 PhD students during 2025–2026. We are looking for highly motivated and talented students with a strong background in at least two areas among AI, wireless communication, mathematical optimization, or Bayesian inference.

If you are interested in joining the research group or know someone who might be, please reach out with your CV, publications if any, and transcripts.

WPI has rolling admission, so students are welcome to apply anytime during the year.

We are actively seeking motivated Ph.D. students passionate about reshaping the future of wireless communications through rigorous research and innovation. If you are excited about working at the intersection of AI, signal processing, and wireless systems, we welcome you to join us.
